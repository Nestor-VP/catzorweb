    Commands

    Create a new clan leaderboard

    1. Type /addclan ClanTag ( ex. /addclan ita01)

    2. Check the results , if the resulting details match your clan information, then press "addclan".

    3. A new text-channel will be created, the bot will print your clan leaderboard on that channel.

    4. You can rename the text-channel as you wish.

    5. the bot will update your leaderboard each hour.
